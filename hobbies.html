Thoughts on Kangaroos

What do they do?

Kangaroos are a people of many hobbies. They usually like to spend their time hopping in fields, terrorising pedestrians and the like.

Their favourite pastime however, is the ancient art of boxing. Many male kangaroos see being an excellent boxer as the rite of passage into manhood. Kangaroos are also renowned for on average being the best boxer (in all weight divisions) animal in the world

They can be found fighting over many things, usually it will be over females or territory, but most kangaroos are known to have a great interest in the KFC (Kangaroo Fighting Championship)
