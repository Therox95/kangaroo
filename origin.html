Thoughts on Kangaroos

Where did they come from?

There are many myths of how kangaroos originated. Some believe they are the chosen race destined to lead us to freedom.

Others believe they are a result of alien experiments in an attempt to cross Muhammed Ali with a Rabbit

The most popular belief however is that they are actually the closest living relative to the ancient velociraptor. Experts from around the world have gathered fossil samples to support this.
